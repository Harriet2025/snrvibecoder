About Accra Technical University

Pioneering technical education in Ghana since 1961

Who We Are

Accra Technical University (ATU) is one of Ghana's leading technical universities dedicated to providing high-quality technical and vocational education. The university focuses on innovation, practical skills development, and industry collaboration to prepare students for the modern workforce.

Established to promote technical excellence, ATU offers diploma, higher national diploma (HND), and degree programmes across various disciplines including engineering, applied sciences, business, computing, and creative arts.

Our History

A legacy of excellence in technical education spanning over six decades

1961

Founded as Accra Technical Institute

Established to provide technical and vocational training to support Ghana's industrial development after independence.

1993

Upgraded to Polytechnic Status

Became Accra Polytechnic under the PNDC Law 321, expanding academic offerings and student enrollment.

2016

Converted to Technical University

Transformed into Accra Technical University under the Technical Universities Act (Act 922), enabling the award of degree programmes.

Today

A Leading Technical University

ATU now offers over 50 programmes across multiple faculties, serving over 10,000 students with world-class facilities and industry partnerships.
